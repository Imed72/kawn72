About

I’m Imed Belarouci, founder of KOWN Seven — an aerospace startup on a mission to make space access affordable, reusable, and clean. Our core product is CRAMARCSS, a revolutionary hybrid air-breathing rocket engine that merges high efficiency with low complexity for hypersonic propulsion.

Technology - CRAMARCSS

CRAMARCSS is currently in the patenting process with the USPTO. It is designed for single-stage-to-orbit reusable spaceplanes, drastically reducing launch costs and eliminating space debris.

Vision

Our long-term goal is to develop a deep space logistics and propulsion system based on fuel recycling — unlocking large-scale space industry. We also plan to integrate AI, robotics, and human remote control to support sustainable space colonization.
