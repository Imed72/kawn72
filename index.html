About

I’m Imed Belarouci, founder of KAWN Seven — an aerospace startup on a mission to make space access affordable and debris-free . Our core product is CRAMARCSS, a revolutionary hybrid air-breathing rocket engine that merges high efficiency with low complexity for hypersonic propulsion.

Technology - CRAMARCSS

CRAMARCSS is currently undergoing the patenting process with the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO). Designed specifically for Single-Stage-to-Orbit (SSTO) reusable spaceplanes, this innovative propulsion system aims to drastically reduce launch costs while eliminating space debris, supporting a more sustainable future in space access. While detailed technical specifications will be disclosed following the patent grant, CRAMARCSS can be described as a dual-flow engine primarily based on ramjet technology. It extends the traditional ramjet’s operating range and efficiency through the integration of advanced thermal management and cooling technologies, enabling high performance across a broader range of altitudes and velocities.

Vision

The long-term goal is to develop a deep space logistics and propulsion system based on fuel recycling — unlocking large-scale space industry. We also plan to integrate AI, robotics, and human remote control to support sustainable space colonization.
